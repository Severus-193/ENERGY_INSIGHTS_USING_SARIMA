Welcome to our Esteemed Organistaion

Our team of experts combines technical knowledge with a passion for energy efficiency to develop tools that are not only functional but also intuitive and easy to use. Our tools are designed with you in mind, ensuring ease of use and actionable insights. We are dedicated to promoting energy efficiency and contributing to a greener future. We are dedicated to provide innovative solutions for energy management and consumption.

MAROON Model

Maroon Prediction Model is a powerful tool designed to give you a clear and actionable view of your energy usage. This interactive interface allows you to visualize your data, track your progress, and implement effective energy-saving strategies.

SARIMA Chatbot

Meet our AI-driven chatbot WattWatcher, a dedicated assistant for all your energy-related inquiries. Our chatbot is equipped with the latest technology to provide you with personalized advice on reducing power consumption

Bill Calculator

Our calculator not only shows you how much you owe but also explains the cost structure, including the rates for different usage tiers. This transparency helps you better manage your energy expenses and identify areas where you can save.

Custom Dataset

This model allows you to generate tailored datasets based on specific parameters, helping you gain deeper insights into your energy consumption patterns. Our tool provides you with the flexibility to create datasets that meet your exact requirements.

OUR SERVICES

Maroon Model

The Maroon model is designed to forecast power consumption for different appliances in multiple houses. It leverages historical energy usage data and utilizes time series forecasting techniques such as SARIMA to estimate future energy demands.The predictions help users understand their energy usage trends and optimize their consumption to reduce costs. The output of the model includes estimated power usage for each appliance and overall house consumption, which can be used for efficient energy management.

SARIMA Chatbot

The chatbot serves as an interactive assistant that provides users with insights into their power consumption patterns and offers actionable recommendations to save energy. It can analyze energy usage trends and suggest ways to reduce consumption by identifying peak hours and advising on minimizing appliance usage during those times.The chatbot is integrated into the web interface, allowing users to interact with it easily and get instant feedback on their energy consumption.

Bill Calculator

The bill calculator helps users understand their electricity costs by computing charges based on power consumption. It categorizes energy usage into different slabs and applies respective tariff rates to calculate the total bill.It additionally suggests cost-saving strategies by highlighting potential reductions in usage during peak tariff hours. This feature ensures transparency in billing and empowers users to take informed actions to minimize their electricity expenses.

Custom Dataset

The dataset creation model generates synthetic power consumption data for different houses based on predefined appliance usage patterns. It produces daily and hourly datasets that reflect realistic variations in energy usage, considering factors such as weekends, weekdays, and seasonal changes. The model assigns random but controlled values for each appliance's consumption . These datasets are stored as CSV files and serve as input for the prediction model and chatbot.

OUR SOLUTIONS

Maroon Prediction Model

Our Maroon Prediction Model is a powerful tool designed to give you a clear and actionable view of your energy usage. With real-time data and historical trends, you can easily monitor the percentage of energy consumed by different appliances, identify high-usage patterns, and make informed decisions to optimize your energy consumption. This interactive interface allows you to visualize your data, track your progress, and implement effective energy-saving strategies.

SARIMA Chatbot

Meet our AI-driven chatbot, a dedicated assistant for all your energy-related inquiries. Our chatbot is equipped with the latest technology to provide you with personalized advice on reducing power consumption, understanding your electricity bill, and more. Whether you need tips on minimizing appliance usage during peak hours or a detailed explanation of your billing structure, our chatbot is here to assist you 24/7 with clear, actionable responses.

Data Creation Model

Our Data Creation Model offers a unique solution for users who need customized datasets for analysis and forecasting. This model allows you to generate tailored datasets based on specific parameters, helping you gain deeper insights into your energy consumption patterns. Whether you are conducting research or developing predictive models, our tool provides you with the flexibility to create datasets that meet your exact requirements.

Bill Calculator

Understanding your electricity bill can be complex, but our Bill Calculator simplifies the process. By inputting your energy consumption data, you can quickly receive a detailed breakdown of your bill. Our calculator not only shows you how much you owe but also explains the cost structure, including the rates for different usage tiers. This transparency helps you better manage your energy expenses and identify areas where you can save.
